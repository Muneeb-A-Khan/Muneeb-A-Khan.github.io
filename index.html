About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Pervasive Intelligence (Pi) Lab at Sangmyung University, advised by Dr. Heemin Park. My research focuses on developing efficient and interpretable deep learning frameworks, particularly for computer vision applications such as medical imaging analysis, artifact removal in mobile imaging, object detection, and traffic sign recognition. I am passionate about leveraging machine learning to solve real-world problems and advancing the field through rigorous research and development.

Research Interests

  • Deep Learning & Machine Learning
  • Medical Image Analysis (MRI, X-Ray)
  • Explainable AI (XAI)
  • Image/Video Enhancement & Artifact Removal
  • Object Detection and Tracking
  • Efficient Model Design
